# Problem Statement

## 📌 Background

Accessing government welfare schemes in India is often a complex and fragmented process. Information about schemes is distributed across multiple platforms, making it difficult for citizens to navigate and understand their eligibility.

## ⚠️ Problem

Currently, users must manually:

* Search for relevant schemes across different websites
* Interpret complex eligibility criteria
* Verify and organize required documents

This process is inefficient and challenging, especially for individuals with limited technical knowledge or access to reliable information.

Moreover, there is no unified system that can:

* Automatically determine eligibility
* Extract and verify user data from documents
* Provide clear, actionable guidance

## 📉 Impact

Due to these challenges:

* Many eligible individuals fail to apply for schemes
* Application processes become error-prone
* Users face unnecessary delays and confusion

This leads to underutilization of government welfare initiatives.

## 💡 Proposed Solution

SchemeSense is an AI-powered web platform designed to simplify and automate the process of identifying eligible government schemes.

The system will:

* Allow users to upload personal documents
* Extract relevant information using OCR
* Evaluate eligibility using a rule-based engine
* Provide a list of applicable schemes
* Highlight missing or incomplete documents

By leveraging AI and structured data processing, SchemeSense aims to make government services more accessible, transparent, and efficient.
